What are Pixels?

Pixels are the tiny squares that make up pictures on computers, phones, and TVs. Every picture or video you see is made of thousands or even millions of these tiny squares. Each pixel can be a different color, and when they all come together, they form the images you see. The more pixels there are, the clearer the picture looks.
