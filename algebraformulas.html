Algebra Formulas

1. Slope Formula: \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \)
2. Quadratic Formula: \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \)
3. Point-Slope Form: \( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \)
4. Standard Form of a Line: \( Ax + By = C \)
5. Slope-Intercept Form: \( y = mx + b \)
6. Pythagorean Theorem: \( a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \)
7. Factoring a Quadratic: \( ax^2 + bx + c = (mx + p)(nx + q) \)
8. Distance Formula: \( d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} \)
9. Midpoint Formula: \( M = \left( \frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2} \right) \)
10. Simple Interest Formula: \( I = Prt \)
11. Exponent Rules (Product of Powers): \( a^m \times a^n = a^{m+n} \)
12. Exponent Rules (Power of a Power): \( (a^m)^n = a^{mn} \)
13. Exponent Rules (Quotient of Powers): \( \frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n} \)
14. Distributive Property: \( a(b + c) = ab + ac \)
15. Absolute Value Equation: \( |x| = a \rightarrow x = a \text{ or } x = -a \)
16. Zero Product Property: \( ab = 0 \rightarrow a = 0 \text{ or } b = 0 \)
