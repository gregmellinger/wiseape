Geometry Formulas

1. Area of a Triangle: \( A = \frac{1}{2} \times b \times h \)
2. Pythagorean Theorem: \( a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \)
3. Area of a Circle: \( A = \pi r^2 \)
4. Circumference of a Circle: \( C = 2 \pi r \)
5. Area of a Rectangle: \( A = l \times w \)
6. Perimeter of a Rectangle: \( P = 2l + 2w \)
7. Area of a Parallelogram: \( A = b \times h \)
8. Volume of a Rectangular Prism: \( V = l \times w \times h \)
9. Volume of a Cylinder: \( V = \pi r^2 h \)
10. Volume of a Sphere: \( V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \)
11. Volume of a Cone: \( V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h \)
12. Volume of a Pyramid: \( V = \frac{1}{3} \times B \times h \) where \( B \) is the area of the base
13. Surface Area of a Sphere: \( A = 4 \pi r^2 \)
14. Surface Area of a Rectangular Prism: \( A = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh \)
15. Surface Area of a Cylinder: \( A = 2\pi r h + 2\pi r^2 \)
